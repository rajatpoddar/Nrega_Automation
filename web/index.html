How to Use the Dashboard

Follow these simple instructions for each automation feature.

1. Muster Roll (MR) Generator

  1. Enter your full Panchayat name (e.g., KASRAYDIH).
  2. Fill in the 'From' and 'To' dates.
  3. Enter the exact Designation and Technical Staff names as they appear on the website.
  4. Paste one or more Work Codes into the text box. To run for all available works, leave this box empty.
  5. Click "Start Generation". PDFs will be saved to your Downloads folder.

2. MR Fill & Absent

  1. Enter the Panchayat name.
  2. Paste the list of Work Codes to process.
  3. Click "Start Filling".
  4. The app will open each MSR, mark day 7 absent (if present), then pause and wait.
  5. Make any manual edits in Chrome, click "Save", and handle the browser alerts. The app will automatically detect this and move to the next item.

3. MSR Processor

  1. First, manually navigate to the MSR Payment page in the launched Chrome browser.
  2. Enter the Panchayat name exactly as it appears on the website.
  3. Paste the list of Work Codes to process.
  4. Click "Start Processing". The results will appear in the "Results" tab within the app.

4. FTO Generation

  1. Enter your User ID in the app.
  2. Click "Start FTO Generation".
  3. The automation will navigate to the login page. Enter your password and any captcha directly in the browser window to proceed.
  4. The app will then automatically visit the required verification pages and process all entries.
  5. Generated FTO numbers will appear in the "Results" tab within the app.

5. Wagelist Generator

  1. Manually navigate to the "Send MSR to PO" page in Chrome.
  2. Enter the Agency name (e.g., BURKUNDI) without "Gram Panchayat -".
  3. Click "Start Generation" to process all available items.

6. Send Wagelist

  1. Manually navigate to the "Send Wagelist For Payment" page and select the Financial Year.
  2. Enter the Start and End Row numbers for the checkboxes you want to select on the page.
  3. Click "Start Sending". The app will process each wagelist in the dropdown one by one.

7. Jobcard Verification & Photo

  1. Enter the Panchayat and Village name.
  2. Click "Select Photo Folder" and choose a folder containing photos named after the job card suffix (e.g., `417.jpg` for `.../417`).
  3. If a specific photo isn't found, the default `jobcard.jpeg` will be used.
  4. Click "Start Verification" to process all job cards for the selected village.

8. eMB Entry

  1. Fill in all the configuration details like Panchayat, MB No., Page No., etc.
  2. Paste the list of Work Codes to process.
  3. Click "Start Automation". The app will fill and save the MB Entry form for each work code.

9. Workcode Generator (Abua)

Instructions

  1. Prepare a CSV file with 5 columns in this order: `priority`, `work_name`, `khata_no`, `plot_no`, `village_name`.
  2. In the app, enter the Panchayat name.
  3. Click "Select workcode_data.csv" and choose your file.
  4. Click "Start Automation" to generate the work codes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

Is my NREGA data safe?

Absolutely. The application works by automating your local Chrome browser. It only reads what's on the screen to perform its tasks. None of your NREGA data, work codes, or personal information is ever sent to our servers.

What happens after my 30-day free trial ends?

After 30 days, the automation features will be disabled. To continue using the app, you can support the project by making a small donation to receive a monthly or yearly license key.

How do I get a license key after donating?

After making a donation to our UPI ID, please email the payment screenshot to rajatpoddar@outlook.com. We will verify your payment and email back your personal license key, which you can then enter into the app.

Can I use one license key on multiple computers?

No. Each license key (both trial and paid) is locked to the first computer it is activated on to prevent abuse. If you change your computer, please contact support, and we can reset the binding for your key.

Why do I need to launch Chrome from the app?

The app launches Chrome in a special "remote debugging" mode. This allows the application to securely connect to the browser and automate the data entry tasks. Your regular Chrome browser will not work for automation.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the NREGA Automation Dashboard ("the Application") handles your information. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

1. Information We Collect

The Application is designed to be privacy-focused. We only collect the absolute minimum information required for the Application to function:

  • License Key: The unique key you use to activate the Application.
  • Machine ID: A non-personally identifiable, anonymous hardware identifier (your computer's MAC address) used to lock your license key to a single device.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

We want to be crystal clear about what we don't do. The Application **DOES NOT** collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or sensitive NREGA data. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your NREGA login credentials.
  • Work codes, muster roll numbers, or any data you enter into the NREGA website.
  • Names, job card numbers, or any beneficiary information.
  • Any files on your computer.

All automation tasks are performed locally on your computer by controlling your browser. The data you see and work with on the NREGA website is never sent to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • License Validation: To verify that you have a valid trial or paid license to use the Application.
  • Preventing Abuse: To ensure that a single license key is not used on multiple computers simultaneously, as per our terms of use.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. Your license key and machine ID are transmitted securely to our validation server. We use standard industry practices to protect our server and the data stored on it.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: rajatpoddar@outlook.com
