How to Use the App

Follow these simple instructions for each automation feature.

1. Muster Roll (MR) Generator

  1. Enter your full Panchayat name (e.g., KASRAYDIH).
  2. Select the 'From' and 'To' dates using the calendar.
  3. Enter the exact Designation and Technical Staff names.
  4. Paste one or more Work Codes into the text box. To run for all available works, leave this box empty.
  5. Choose an output action (Save as PDF or Print).
  6. Click "Start Automation".

2. MSR Processor

  1. First, manually navigate to the MSR Payment page in the launched browser.
  2. Enter the Panchayat name exactly as it appears on the website.
  3. Paste the list of Work Codes to process.
  4. Click "Start Automation". The results will appear in the "Results" tab.

3. FTO Generation

  1. Enter your User ID in the app (optional, can log in manually).
  2. Click "Start FTO Generation".
  3. The automation will navigate to the login page. Enter your password and any captcha directly in the browser.
  4. The app will then automatically visit the required verification pages and process all entries.

4. Wagelist Generator

  1. Manually navigate to the "Send MSR to PO" page in the browser.
  2. Enter the Agency name (e.g., BURKUNDI) without "Gram Panchayat -".
  3. Click "Start Generation" to process all available items one by one.

5. Send Wagelist

  1. Select the Financial Year in the app.
  2. Enter the Start and End Row numbers for the checkboxes you want to select on the page.
  3. Click "Start Sending". The app will automatically navigate and process each wagelist.

6. Jobcard Verification & Photo

  1. Enter the Panchayat and Village name.
  2. Click "Select Photo Folder" and choose a folder containing photos named after the job card suffix (e.g., `417.jpg` for `.../417`).
  3. If a photo isn't found, the default `jobcard.jpeg` will be used.
  4. Click "Start Verification" to process all job cards for the village.

7. eMB Entry

  1. Fill in all the configuration details like Panchayat, MB No., Page No., etc.
  2. Paste the list of Work Codes to process.
  3. Click "Start Automation" to fill and save the MB Entry form for each work code.

8. Verify ABPS

  1. Enter the Panchayat name.
  2. Optionally, enter a Village name. If you leave this blank, the app will process all villages in that Panchayat.
  3. Click "Start Automation". The bot will check every record, page by page, and save the results.

9. Add Activity

  1. Enter the desired Unit Price and Quantity.
  2. Paste the list of Work Keys (not full work codes) to process.
  3. Click "Start Automation" to add the default activity (ACT105) to each work key.

10. Workcode Extractor

  1. This is a simple utility tool.
  2. Paste any block of text that contains NREGA work codes into the text area.
  3. Click the "Extract Work Codes" button.
  4. A clean, unique list of work codes will be generated and automatically copied to your clipboard.

11. Workcode Generator (Dynamic)

Instructions

  1. Enter the Panchayat name and click "Load Categories from Website".
  2. Make your selections for all dropdowns from top to bottom.
  3. Prepare a CSV file with the 9 required columns (see demo).
  4. Select your CSV file.
  5. Click "Start Automation" to generate work codes based on your selections and CSV data.

Demo CSV Format

priority,work_name,...,total_saplings
Download Demo CSV

12. IF Editor (Dynamic)

Instructions

  1. Prepare a CSV file with 7 columns (see demo). One row for each Activity/Material to add.
  2. In the app, configure the settings for Page 1 and Page 2.
  3. Use the switch to enable/disable the multi-page (convergence) workflow.
  4. Select your CSV file.
  5. Click "Start Automation".

Demo CSV Format

work_code,beneficiary_type,...,quantity

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

Is my NREGA data safe?

Absolutely. The application works by automating your local browser. It only reads what's on the screen to perform its tasks. None of your NREGA data, work codes, or personal information is ever sent to our servers.

What happens after my 30-day free trial ends?

After 30 days, the automation features will be disabled. To continue using the app, you can support the project by purchasing a monthly or yearly license key.

How do I get a license key after purchasing?

After making a payment on our secure license page, your key will be generated instantly and sent to your registered email address.

Can I use one license key on multiple computers?

Each license key is valid for the number of devices you selected during purchase (1, 2, or 3). The license is locked to the first few computers it is activated on. If you need to change a computer, please contact support.

Why do I need to launch a browser from the app?

The app launches a browser (Chrome or Firefox) with a special profile and a connection that allows the application to securely automate data entry tasks. Your regular browser will not work for automation.
