Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: July 25, 2025

Thank you for supporting the NREGA Automation Dashboard. We appreciate your contribution which helps us maintain and improve the software.

Payments made to receive a license key are considered donations to support the project's ongoing development, server costs, and maintenance. Due to the nature of digital goods and the provision of a 30-day free trial period for evaluation, we have a strict no-refund policy.

No Refunds

All payments are final and non-refundable. We provide a fully-functional 30-day free trial of the Application so that you can test its features and ensure it meets your needs before making a donation for a license key. We encourage all users to make use of this trial period to make an informed decision.

Cancellation

Your license key is valid for the duration you selected (e.g., monthly or yearly). There are no recurring subscriptions. Once your license period expires, the Application will simply revert to its disabled state. You are under no obligation to make another payment.

Exceptional Circumstances

In the rare event of a payment error or a technical issue that prevents you from activating a license key you have paid for, please contact our support team immediately. We will work with you to resolve the issue, which may include providing a new key or, in exceptional cases where we are at fault, processing a refund at our sole discretion.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or are facing issues with your license key, please contact us at: rajatpoddar@outlook.com
