Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how NREGA Bot ("the Application") handles your information. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

1. Information We Collect

The Application is designed to be privacy-focused. We only collect the absolute minimum information required for the Application to function:

  • License Key: The unique key you use to activate the Application.
  • Machine ID: A non-personally identifiable, anonymous hardware identifier (your computer's MAC address) used to lock your license key to a single device.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

We want to be crystal clear about what we don't do. The Application **DOES NOT** collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or sensitive NREGA data. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your NREGA login credentials.
  • Work codes, muster roll numbers, or any data you enter into the NREGA website.
  • Names, job card numbers, or any beneficiary information.
  • Any files on your computer.

All automation tasks are performed locally on your computer by controlling your browser. The data you see and work with on the NREGA website is never sent to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • License Validation: To verify that you have a valid trial or paid license to use the Application.
  • Preventing Abuse: To ensure that a single license key is not used on multiple computers simultaneously, as per our terms of use.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. Your license key and machine ID are transmitted securely to our validation server. We use standard industry practices to protect our server and the data stored on it.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: nregabot@gmail.com
